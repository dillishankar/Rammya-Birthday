My Dearest Rammya,

Every birthday you celebrate is a reminder to me of how lucky this world is to have you in it. You are not just a person — you are a feeling. A warm morning. A gentle song that stays with you all day.

There are not enough words in every language I know to describe what your smile does to a room, or what your laughter does to ordinary moments — it turns them into memories I will carry for a lifetime.

Today I want you to know: you are loved beyond measure. You are enough, you are magic, and you deserve every beautiful thing life has to offer.

Happy Birthday, Rammya. Here is to you — every magnificent version of you, past, present, and all the incredible ones still to come.

Forever yours,

With all my love
